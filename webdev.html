I have been working as a web designer and developer since 2001. I’ve had the chance to work on a wide variety of different projects. I’ve listed a bunch of them below; unfortunately much of the stuff I’ve worked on is no longer online, but I do still have screenshots! In cases where the sites are still online, I’ve provided high end web links to whisk you across the internet to view them directly.

Spark Design Co. (2008-2018)

The bulk of my most recent work was done for the company Spark Design Co. On most of these projects, I was responsible for front end and back end coding, building css and html templates and integrating them with customized CMS installations using Expression Engine.

University of Guelph (2006-2007)

I served as a staff Web Designer at the University of Guelph, where I worked on a variety of internal website projects, including a historical walking tour, fundraising initiatives, and updates to the University homepage. Web standards and accessibility were a must.
